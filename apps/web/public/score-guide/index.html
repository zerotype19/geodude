Optiview Score Guide

Optiview uses 21 weighted checks to evaluate how well your site is optimized for AI search engines and LLM citations. Each check is scored 0-3 and contributes to your overall AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) and GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) scores.

This guide explains what we measure, why it matters, and how to improve your scores.

How We Score

Score Scale (0-3)

Score Meaning Weight Applied
3 Strong - Fully optimized 100% of weight
2 Moderate - Partially optimized 66% of weight
1 Weak - Minimal optimization 33% of weight
0 Poor/Missing - Not optimized 0% of weight

AEO Checks (11 checks)

Answer Engine Optimization checks focus on how well your content is structured for featured snippets, AI Overviews, and answer boxes.

Check Weight What We Measure
A1: Answer-First Design 15 Summary block, jump links, or tables above the fold
A2: Topical Cluster 15 Internal links to related content
A3: Site Authority 15 Organization and Author schema
A4: Originality & Effort 12 Tables, data, or 3+ outbound citations
A5: Schema Accuracy 10 Structured data (JSON-LD) present and valid
A6: Crawlability 10 Canonical tags, proper URLs, no duplicate content
A7: UX & Performance 8 No layout shift risk, fast page load
A8: Discoverability 6 Sitemaps present and accessible
A9: Freshness 5 dateModified present in schema
A10: AI Overviews 4 Citations block, chunkable structure for AI summaries
A11: Render Visibility 10 Content visible in static HTML (70%+ for score 3)

GEO Checks (10 checks)

Generative Engine Optimization checks focus on making your content citeable and discoverable by LLMs like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity.

Check Weight What We Measure
G1: Citable Facts 15 Fact blocks, statistics, key takeaways clearly marked
G2: Provenance 15 Author, publisher, citations, license schema present
G3: Evidence Density 12 Citations block + 3+ outbound references to sources
G4: AI Crawler Access 12 GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot allowed in robots.txt
G5: Chunkability 10 Clean HTML structure, semantic markup for easy parsing
G6: Stable URLs 8 Canonical fact URLs with anchors for deep linking
G7: Dataset Links 8 Links to datasets, research, or sources hub
G8: Policy Transparency 6 License and reuse policy clearly stated
G9: Update Hygiene 7 Changelog or version history maintained
G10: Cluster Linking 7 Links to sources hub or related evidence pages

Live Citation Testing

Beyond structural checks, we test whether your brand actually appears in live LLM responses by running real queries against:

  • ChatGPT (OpenAI) - GPT-4 with web browsing
  • Claude (Anthropic) - Claude 3 with web search
  • Perplexity - Real-time web search and citation
  • Brave Search - AI-powered search results

Your GEO Adjusted Score combines structural readiness (70% weight) with real-world citation performance (30% weight).
