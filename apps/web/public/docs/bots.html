Bot Detection & Privacy

Optiview identifies AI bots using User-Agent pattern matching. When a bot visits your site:

  • IP Hashing: We hash visitor IPs with SHA-256 (no raw IPs stored)
  • User-Agent Logging: Full User-Agent string is recorded for classification
  • Visit Timestamp: Date/time of the visit is logged
  • Referrer: HTTP Referer header is captured if present
  • No PII: We do not store cookies, device IDs, or other personal identifiers

Blocking AI Bots

If you want to block specific AI bots, use Disallow in robots.txt:

User-agent: GPTBot
Disallow: /

Note: Blocking AI bots may reduce your content's visibility in AI-powered search and answer engines.
