Audit Checks

Optiview performs comprehensive audits to ensure your website is optimized for AI crawlers and search engines. Here's what we check:

Content Depth: Measures word count to ensure sufficient content (minimum 100 words recommended)

üîí Trust (10% of score)

Page Accessibility: Ensures pages return 200 OK status codes
Load Performance: Tracks page load times to identify slow pages
Error Detection: Identifies broken links, 404s, and server errors

Audit Limits & Guardrails

  • Max Pages: 30 pages per audit
  • Rate Limit: 1 request per second (respectful crawling)
  • User-Agent: OptiviewAuditBot/1.0 (+https://www.optiview.ai)
  • Robots.txt Compliance: We obey Disallow rules in robots.txt

Issue Severity Levels

  • Critical: Major issues that prevent AI crawlers from accessing content (e.g., blocked in robots.txt, missing sitemap)
  • Warning: Important but non-blocking issues (e.g., missing JSON-LD, thin content)
  • Info: Optimization opportunities (e.g., slow load times, minor formatting issues)
