Understanding how AI assistants reference your content and how Optiview tracks it

What are AI Citations?

AI citations occur when AI assistants like Perplexity, ChatGPT Search, Claude, and Brave AI reference your website or specific pages in their responses to user queries. Unlike traditional search engine results, AI citations are integrated into conversational responses, making them highly valuable for brand visibility and traffic generation.
