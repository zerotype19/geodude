How Optiview Scoring Works

Each page on your site is evaluated against 36 diagnostic checks organized into 6 key categories. Your overall score reflects how well your content is structured for AI assistant discovery, understanding, and citation.

  • Page-Level Checks (23): Evaluated individually for each URL
  • Site-Level Checks (13): Aggregated across your entire domain
  • Impact Weighting: High, Medium, and Low priority based on citation influence
