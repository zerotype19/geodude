Report Sections in Detail

1. Executive Summary

High-level overview of your AI visibility performance, including overall composite score, citation rate across AI platforms, and key takeaways. Perfect for leadership briefings.

2. Category Breakdown

Detailed analysis across all 6 scoring categories: Content & Clarity, Technical Foundations, Semantic Structure, Discovery & Navigation, Authority & Trust, and AI Citation Performance. Each category shows:

  • Current score and percentage
  • Number of checks passing vs total
  • Strengths to maintain
  • Opportunities to improve
  • Affected pages requiring attention

3. Priority Fixes

Top 10-15 issues ranked by weighted impact. Each fix includes:

  • Specific problem identified
  • Impact level (High/Medium/Low)
  • Expected score improvement
  • Step-by-step fix instructions
  • Affected pages

4. Citation Analysis

Real-world citation performance across ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity:

  • Overall citation rate by AI source
  • 8-10 successful citation examples (queries where you appear)
  • 8+ missed opportunities (queries where competitors appear but you don't)
  • Top cited pages from your domain
  • Suggested content gaps to fill

5. Site-Level Diagnostics

Site-wide health checks with pass/warn/fail indicators:

  • FAQ coverage across your site
  • Entity graph completeness
  • Robots.txt configuration
  • AI crawler access policies
  • Sitemap quality and coverage

6. Page Insights

Detailed analysis of individual page performance:

  • Top-performing pages (highest scores)
  • Pages needing attention (lowest scores)
  • Quick wins (easy fixes with high impact)
  • Pages being cited in AI responses
