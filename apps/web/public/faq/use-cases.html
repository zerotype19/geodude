Use Cases & Examples

See how real companies across industries use Optiview to improve AI visibility, increase citations, and drive discovery.

E-Commerce

Product FAQs Drive Perplexity Citations

Challenge: A mid-sized e-commerce brand was getting organic traffic but zero citations in AI-powered shopping queries.

Solution: Added FAQ schema to product pages, implemented HowTo structured data for usage guides, and rewrote product descriptions to answer common questions upfront.

3.2x Citation increase
18% Traffic from AI referrals
42 Days to see impact

Key insight: Comparison pages ("X vs Y") with structured data got cited most frequently. AI models favor head-to-head analysis for shopping queries.

Healthcare

Authoritative Content Earns Model Trust

Challenge: A healthcare provider had comprehensive content but AI models cited WebMD and Mayo Clinic instead.

Solution: Added Organization schema with medical credentials, implemented MedicalWebPage schema, cited peer-reviewed sources, and restructured content as Q&A with expert attribution.

5x More AI citations
67% Visibility score increase
8 Weeks to authority gains

Key insight: Medical credentials in Organization schema and clear expert attribution dramatically improved trust signals.

Publishing

FAQ and HowTo Formats Drive ChatGPT Inclusion

Challenge: A B2B publisher had high-quality content but inconsistent AI citations.

Solution: Converted 50+ articles to FAQ format, added HowTo schema for tutorials, implemented Article schema with author attribution, and created "Quick Answer" sections at the top of each post.

89% Of articles now cited
2.1x AI-referred traffic
30 Days to see results

Key insight: "Answer first" formatting (leading with conclusion before context) matched how AI models extract information.

B2B SaaS

Technical Documentation Boosts "Answer Coverage"

Challenge: A SaaS company had comprehensive docs but AI models cited competitors when users asked "how to" questions.

Solution: Restructured documentation with HowTo schema, added code examples in FAQPage format, implemented SoftwareApplication schema for the product, and created integration guides as Q&A.

4.5x Developer query citations
34% Docs traffic from AI
21 Days to see gains

Key insight: Code examples with clear explanations got cited most. AI models favor concrete implementation guidance over abstract concepts.

Common Patterns Across Industries

While implementation varies by industry, successful companies share these traits:

  • Question-based content structure: Every page answers at least one specific question
  • Schema markup adoption: 80%+ of pages have relevant structured data
  • Answer-first formatting: Clear conclusions before supporting details
  • AI crawler access: No robots.txt blocks on training bots
  • Entity graph completeness: Organization schema with logos and social profiles
  • Internal content clusters: Related pages linked together with clear hierarchy
