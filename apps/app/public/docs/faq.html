Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Optiview's AI SEO and Visibility Intelligence platform

What is Optiview?

Optiview is the first AI-powered SEO platform that analyzes your website's visibility across both traditional search engines and AI assistants like Perplexity, ChatGPT Search, and Claude. We provide comprehensive audits, real-time visibility tracking, and actionable recommendations to optimize for the future of search.

How does Visibility Intelligence work?

Our Visibility Intelligence feature tracks how AI assistants reference your content when answering user queries. We monitor citations from Perplexity, ChatGPT Search, and Claude, then generate 0-100 visibility scores based on citation frequency, domain diversity, and recency. This gives you insights into your "AI findability" that traditional SEO tools can't provide.

What makes Optiview different from other SEO tools?

Unlike traditional SEO tools that only analyze search engine optimization, Optiview is the first platform to track visibility across AI assistants. We provide:

  • Multi-assistant citation tracking (Perplexity, ChatGPT, Claude)
  • Real-time visibility scores and rankings
  • AI-specific optimization recommendations
  • Competitive intelligence across AI platforms
  • Traditional SEO audits with modern AI insights

How accurate are the AI assistant citations?

We use live API integrations with Perplexity, OpenAI's ChatGPT Search, and Anthropic's Claude to capture real citations as they happen. Our system processes actual AI responses and extracts URLs using both native API responses and heuristic parsing for maximum accuracy.

What types of audits does Optiview perform?

Optiview performs comprehensive SEO audits covering:

  • Crawlability: Technical SEO, robots.txt, sitemaps, page speed
  • Structured Data: JSON-LD validation, schema markup, entity optimization
  • Answerability: Content quality, FAQ optimization, snippet readiness
  • Trust & Authority: E-E-A-T factors, security, credibility signals
  • AI Visibility: Multi-assistant citation tracking and scoring

How often is visibility data updated?

Visibility Intelligence data is updated in real-time as AI assistants generate new citations. Our system:

  • Processes new citations every 5 minutes
  • Generates daily visibility scores and rankings
  • Updates weekly competitive analysis
  • Provides real-time filtering and export capabilities
